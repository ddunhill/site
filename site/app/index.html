Hello

I am a full stack web developer based both in London and Glasgow.

Since starting in 1995, my career has been mainly with the Microsoft technologies, building web sites and data services used by online and mobile applications. For the last 12 years, I have been working in the advertising industry on a range of projects for a broad selection of household name clients. These projects have included real time social media score boards at Wimbledon and marketing campaigns for companies like Nescafe, Vodafone and Sony.

My drivers are getting to understand how a system or technology works and the sense of achievement at finding a neat solution to a tricky problem.

Triathlon calculator - Performance predictor and results graphs

.NET example - Using google graphs the page displays the results of races I have done plus predictions of upcoming ones. The data is stored in a Google doc which is accessed via through OAuth and the Google API. The future race results are estimated based on the existing results and linear curve fitting.
